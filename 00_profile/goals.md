# Goals

What this site is for. The `growth-marketer` and `content-strategist` optimize toward these.

## Primary audience
1. Recruiters / hiring managers (internships, research roles).
2. Startup founders & collaborators.
3. Researchers / academics.

## Success metrics
1. Become searchable as "Ed Chen + EECS at MIT."
2. A visitor comes away thinking: "special candidate with unique insights — worth reaching out to."
3. Perspectives posts add value beyond the résumé (show judgment, not just output).

## SEO / GEO targets
- Own name + field for search engines.
- Accurate `schema.org` Person data so generative engines cite affiliation/role correctly.
- Keep a current sitemap; submit to Google Search Console.

## Content cadence
- Online CV / Perspectives: ≥ 1 piece per quarter, milestone-triggered.
- Personal Hub: as often as there's something useful to say (bilingual OK).

## Distribution
- Cross-post new Perspectives to LinkedIn with a link back here.
- Keep GitHub profile README pointing here.

## Constraints
- PhD completion comes first (RQE Dec 2026, graduation ~Jun 2027). The site is secondary;
  quiet quarters are acceptable, year-long silence is not.
