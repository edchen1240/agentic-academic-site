# Brand

How the site should look and sound. The `web-designer` reads this for `brand-to-css`;
the `content-strategist` reads it for voice.

## Voice & tone
- Clear, direct, builder-minded. Substance over hype.
- Confident but not boastful; let work speak.
- Plain language; explain jargon when it appears.

## Visual identity
- Default theme: dark, with a one-click light toggle.
- Accent color: green (`#48DA9B` dark / `#0f7d6f` light). Change in `01_www/css/style.css` `:root`.
- Typography: Lato (300/400 weights), generous whitespace, justified body text.
- Layout: fixed sidebar (photo + contact + social) beside a content column; circle-divider motif between sections.
- Feel: a serious-but-approachable research portfolio. "MIT clean," not flashy.

## Do
- Keep navigation minimal; remove a tab rather than leave a dead link.
- Maintain bilingual parity (EN ⇄ 繁中) if the second language is kept.

## Don't
- No autoplay, no popups, no heavy frameworks.
- No stock-photo filler; use real photos or none.
